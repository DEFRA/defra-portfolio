About the digital portfolio

The digital portfolio belongs to the Department for Work & Pensions (DWP) Business Transformation Group (BTG).

How to get something added to the portfolio

  1. Contact the portfolio team
  2. Work with them to define the problem you want to solve
  3. They will help you finalise an Discovery Approval Document
  4. This document is then presented to the BTG management team who decide whether the work is added to the backlog and its priority

What happens next

The BTG Challenge and Support forum will bring your work out of the backlog when the right people become available.
